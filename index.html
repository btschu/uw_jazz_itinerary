Expectations

This is an officially approved event organized by South High School. As such, all students are required to adhere to the school's behavior code and dress code. If any student is suspected of or found to be in possession of drugs or alcohol, immediate notification will be made to the local authorities. Please keep in mind that we are ambassadors of South High School throughout the trip, and therefore, it is crucial to exhibit appropriate behavior at all times.
